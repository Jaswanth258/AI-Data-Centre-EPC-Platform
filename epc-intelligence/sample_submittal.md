## 3. FABRICATION APPROACH

### 3.1 Shop Drawings
- Shop drawings will be submitted **14 calendar days** prior to fabrication commencement.
- Drawings will include member sizes, connection details, bolt patterns, and erection marks.
- Note: Weld symbols and camber requirements will be included in a supplementary drawing set submitted separately.

### 3.2 Dimensional Tolerances
- Member length tolerance: ±3 mm for members ≤ 9 m; ±6 mm for members > 9 m
- Column plumb tolerance: H/1000 or 25 mm maximum
- Beam camber tolerance: ±15 mm from specified camber

### 3.3 Welding
- All welding performed by certified welders. Certification is per company internal qualification standard.
- Weld Procedure Specifications (WPS) will be submitted 7 days before fabrication.
- Full Penetration Welds (CJP) at primary moment connections — confirmed.
- **NDT:** 100% UT for CJP welds. Fillet weld inspection will be conducted by visual inspection only.

### 3.4 Surface Preparation and Coating
- Steel surfaces will be blast cleaned to **SSPC-SP 3 (Power Tool Cleaning)** prior to priming.
- **Primer:** Zinc-rich epoxy primer — 60 microns DFT
- **Finish coat:** High-build epoxy — 125 microns DFT (exposed areas)
- Field weld touch-up will be completed before final handover inspection.

## 4. TESTING AND INSPECTION PLAN

### 4.1 Mill Testing
- CMTRs to be provided for 90% of primary member heats at time of shipment.
- Third-party CMTR verification not planned; internal QC review to be conducted instead.

### 4.2 Dimensional Inspection
- 100% dimensional inspection of primary members will be carried out.
- Inspection reports will be submitted to the Engineer within **10 business days** of inspection completion.

### 4.3 Coating Inspection
- DFT measured per SSPC-PA 1 using calibrated gauges.
- Minimum 3 readings per 10 m² of surface area.

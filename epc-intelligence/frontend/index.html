Root Cause

Engineers manually cross-reference vendor submittals (PDFs with hundreds of technical parameters) against ground-truth specifications (IEC, ANSI, project-specific docs). A single missed non-conformance — like 8-minute battery autonomy instead of the required 10 minutes — can cause months of rework, equipment rejection, and schedule delays worth millions.

Our Solution

AegisEPC is an AI-powered project intelligence layer that automates specification retrieval, RFI resolution, and vendor submittal compliance verification in seconds — not days.

Technical Innovation

Clause-Tree Structural Retrieval

Unlike standard RAG that chunks documents into arbitrary 500-token blocks — cutting clauses mid-sentence and losing hierarchical context — AegisEPC parses document headers into a deterministic clause tree (Section → Clause X.Y). The LLM navigates the table of contents first, then fetches exact ground-truth clause text. Result: zero chunk-truncation, 100% verifiable citations, and exact clause references.

Traditional Vector RAG

  • Chunks cut across clause boundaries
  • Cosine similarity misses exact matches
  • No structural awareness of sections
  • Hallucination-prone on technical data

AegisEPC Clause-Tree

  • Preserves document hierarchy exactly
  • LLM navigates TOC, then fetches clause
  • Deterministic, explainable retrieval
  • Zero embeddings, zero vector DB needed
